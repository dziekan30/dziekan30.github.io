Here is my Personal Project

Sniffer

It's an adoption app for dogs. You can adopt a dog, also you can post a dog for adoption. I use Ruby on Rails for back-end, and Vue.js for front-end. In this app you can request adoption, the owner of the dog will be able to see it and also approve you or not. I added twilio to that soo owner also will receive a text message, whenever someone will send a request. In the text message will be the name of the dog and name of the user who wants to adopt a dog.
